Alien Infiltration

Instead of doing the Lundum Dare competition since I was not inspired at all, I made a small little game in a day called Alien Infiltration. It's one of those games that's very simple to pick up, but ramps up in difficulty fairly quickly. You just have to dodge incoming missiles as an invading flying saucer, but that's no easy task. The missiles start coming faster and faster and move much more quickly aswell. You can get this one on Google Play.
